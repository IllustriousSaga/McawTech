W.A.R.D Security

W.A.R.D Security

Description

WARD stands for weapon analyzation, recognition, and detection. WARD is our artificial Intelligence security system and detects any dangers to around your house. Once it sees an object, WARD scans the surroundings and compares them to a dataset of a variety of objects to determine what object is in the surroundings. If it scans anything of danger, such as a firearm, it will alert the home owners that a person with a firearm is approaching their house.
